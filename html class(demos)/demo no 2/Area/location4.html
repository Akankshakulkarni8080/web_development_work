Ahmednagar is a city in the Ahmednagar district, Maharashtra, India, about 120 km northeast of Pune and 114 km from Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar. It is the seat of its namesake district. Ahmednagar has several dozen buildings and sites from the Nizam Shahi period.
